A Sweet History

Created in 1930 by Mars, Incorporated, Snickers was named after the Mars family's favorite horse. The combination of nougat, peanuts, caramel, and milk chocolate quickly became a worldwide sensation.

Today, Snickers is sold in more than 95 countries and generates global sales exceeding $2 billion annually, making it one of the most successful candy bars ever created.
